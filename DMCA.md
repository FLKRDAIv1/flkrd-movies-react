# DMCA Copyright Takedown Procedures

**FKURD MOVIES** acts strictly as a clientside browser crawler indexing public metadata. We respect copyright properties and comply with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act ("DMCA").

If you are a copyright holder or authorized representative and believe that indexed link results display unauthorized materials, you may file a structured notification by emailing:

**legal@fkurd.pro**

Your notice must include:
1. Identification of the copyrighted work.
2. The specific URL path/identifier containing the link to be removed.
3. Your contact address, email, and telephone number.
4. A signed statement declaring good faith belief of copyright infringement.

Structured notifications are processed and removed within 24 hours.
